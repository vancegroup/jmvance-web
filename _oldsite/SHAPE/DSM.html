Direct Shape Manipulation in a Virtual Environment


The car shell is represented as a NURBS surface. The direct manipulation approach for NURBS free form deformation is used to allow the designer to "push" (A) or "pull" (B) on the surface to change the shape. An image of the user's hand can be seen in the virtual environment and corresponds to the user's real hand motion which is detected by the Cyberglove sensors.
