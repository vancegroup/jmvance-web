Abstract
 
This paper presents an overview of the current activities of the Women in Engineering Leadership Institute (WELI) which are being funded through a National Science Foundation ADVANCE Grant. Details on these activities are given, along with an overview of conference assessment to date. In addition, future plans for the organization are discussed.
