Creating a Virtual Reality Environment for Spherical Mechanism Design


Pierre M. Larochelle
Florida Institute of Technology

Judy M. Vance
Iowa State University

J. Michael McCarthy
University of California, Irvine

1998 NSF Design and Manufacturing Grantees Conference, Monterrey, Mexico, January 5-8, 1998, pp. 83-84

Abstract
In this paper we present a summary of our continuing efforts to create a virtual reality (VR) environment for the design of spherical mechanisms. This VR design environment is based upon new and existing state-of-the-art results in the fields of applied VR and spatial mechanism design. It is our hope that this project will result in: (1) a new paradigm for utilizing VR techniques in engineering design, and (2) an innovative tool that will facilitate the design of spherical mechanisms.
