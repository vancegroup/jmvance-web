Virtual Reality Techniques to Improve Product Design

Judy M. Vance
Virtual Reality Applications Center
Iowa State University
Ames, Iowa 50011-2160

NSF Design and Manufacturing Grantees Conference Proceedings, January 3-6, 2000, Vancouver, British Columbia, CD-ROM

Abstract
This award supports an investigation of the use of virtual reality in product design. This past year the focus of the work performed included investigating the use of virtual reality in both virtual prototyping and engineering analysis areas of the product development process. Two applications were developed: a virtual environment for performing free-form surface deformation and a virtual environment for interacting with computational fluid dynamics results.
